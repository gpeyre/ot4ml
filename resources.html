References and software

A compact portal to books, monographs, long reviews, surveys, and software libraries that support the OT4ML book and its computational examples.

Books and Monographs

Theory

Classical optimal transport

  • Mass Transportation Problems, Rachev and Rüschendorf.
  • Topics in Optimal Transportation, Villani.
  • Optimal Transport: Old and New, Villani.
  • Optimal Transport for Applied Mathematicians, Santambrogio.
Geometry

Flows and economics

  • Gradient Flows in Metric Spaces and in the Space of Probability Measures, Ambrosio, Gigli and Savaré.
  • Optimal Transport Methods in Economics, Galichon.
  • Statistical Optimal Transport, Chewi, Niles-Weed and Rigollet.

  • A User's Guide to Optimal Transport, Ambrosio and Gigli.
  • A Review of Matrix Scaling and Sinkhorn's Normal Form, Idel.
ML and related fields

Modern long reviews

  • A Survey of the Schrödinger Problem, Léonard.
  • Scalable Optimal Transport Methods in Machine Learning, Khamis et al.
  • Recent Advances in Optimal Transport for Machine Learning, Montesuma, Ngolè Mboula and Souloumiac.
  • A Survey of Optimal Transport for Computer Graphics and Computer Vision, Bonneel, Digne, Feydy and Peyré.
